# Dinner Card - Sunday
**Label:** Sample Meal Prep Option
**Recipe:** Air-Fryer Salmon
**Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 12 min | **Total:** ~17 min
## Pull Out
- 2 (6-oz) salmon fillets — skin-on or skinless (frozen works, see notes)
- Olive oil, as needed
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 lemon (optional)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes (optional)
## Thawing
- Thaw overnight in fridge, or run under cold water 10–15 min before cooking. Pat extra dry before oiling.
## Cook
- Heat air fryer to 400°F (if preheating is recommended for your model).
- Pat salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Brush olive oil all over salmon; season with salt and pepper.
- **If using lemon and tomatoes:** Thinly slice half the lemon crosswise; set remaining half aside. In a small bowl, toss cherry tomatoes and lemon slices with 1 tbsp olive oil and a pinch of salt.
