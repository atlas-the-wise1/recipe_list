# Dinner Card - Friday
**Label:** Meal Prep Option
**Recipe:** Roasted Turkey Meatballs With Mozzarella and Scallions
**Total Time:** About 32 minutes
**Energy Level:** Medium
**Cleanup:** Light
## Before Work or the Night Before
- No advance prep needed.
## Pull Out
- ½ cup crushed buttery crackers (like Ritz)
- 1 large egg
- 1½ tsp ground cumin
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 6 scallions, coarsely chopped (reserve some for garnish)
- 2 jalapeños (optional), seeded if desired, coarsely chopped (reserve some for garnish)
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 2 oz chopped mozzarella, preferably fresh (~½ cup)
## Cook
1. Heat oven to **425°F**. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, stir together cracker crumbs, egg, cumin, 1 tsp salt, and a few grinds of pepper until a paste forms. Stir in scallions and jalapeños (if using), reserving a little of each for garnish.
3. Add ground turkey and mix with your hands until combined. Add mozzarella and mix just until incorporated. Mixture will be loose and sticky.
4. Using wet hands, roll into **12 meatballs** (about 3 tbsp each) and place on the prepared sheet pan.
5. Bake until cooked through and bottoms are golden, **13–17 minutes**.
6. Garnish with reserved scallions and jalapeños if desired. Serve warm.
## While It Cooks
- Storage: 4 days fridge / 3 months freezer — freeze on sheet pan first then transfer to a bag
- Put away unused ingredients.
