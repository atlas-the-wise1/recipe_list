## 📦 Meal Prep Guide

### **Monday (MEAL PREP DAY 1)**
Batch-cook **Bricklayer-Style Nachos (Healthier)** — makes enough for:
- Monday lunch
- Friday lunch (reheat or assemble fresh)

**Prep Steps:**
1. Brown ground turkey + turkey chorizo (20 min)
2. Prepare tomato sauce (15 min)
3. Sauté onions & jalapeños (10 min)
4. Assemble nachos on baked tortilla chips
5. Top with cheese & avocado just before serving (don't prep ahead)
6. Store meat sauce & toppings separately in containers (fridge, 4 days)

**Storage:** Meat sauce → 4 days fridge | Cheese/toppings → separate | Assemble fresh day-of for best texture

---

### **Thursday (MEAL PREP DAY 2)**
Batch-cook **Rotisserie Chicken & Greens Pasta (Healthier)** — makes enough for:
- Thursday dinner
- Sunday lunch (as creamy doenjang pasta repurpose OR reheat pasta)

**Prep Steps:**
1. Buy rotisserie chicken (5 min) OR roast chicken at home (45 min)
2. Cook pasta al dente (10 min)
3. Sauté greens in garlic + olive oil (8 min)
4. Combine with creaminess element (light cream or nutritional yeast), lemon zest
5. Portion into 2 containers
6. Refrigerate up to 4 days

**Pro tip:** Cook extra rotisserie chicken thighs on Thursday to use for:
- Sunday dinner (spaghetti with meat sauce can use shredded chicken variant)
- Quick snacks throughout week
