# Wedding Cut Cookbook

Curated from the highest-signal recipes in the library.

## Rules

- `healthy` recipes are the default weekly picks.
- `balanced` recipes are fallback options when a slot needs variety.
- `treat` recipes stay out of the default rotation.
- The planner can randomize by day or by week, then generate groceries automatically.

## Weekly Use

- Pick one breakfast anchor, one lunch anchor, and two or three quick dinners for the week.
- Let the weekly planner generate the full day-by-day menu and grocery list from the selected pool.
- If a dinner needs to be especially fast, prefer the quick-dinner pool first.
