# Clovis Farms Organic Super Smoothie

**Category:** B — Breakfast
**Tags:** `#heart-healthy` `#low-cholesterol` `#quick` `#fat-loss` `#vegetarian`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~215 | ~16g | ~1g | ~38g | ~7g | ~5mg |
**Health Score:** 8/10

**Serves:** 1
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 Clovis Farms Organic Super Smoothie pouch
- 1/2 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
- 1 3.7 oz serving Sofresco B-Tox beetroot & ginger juice
- 1/2 cup ice

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Add the smoothie pouch, Greek yogurt, beetroot & ginger juice serving, and ice to a blender.

**STEP 2:** Blend on HIGH until smooth and creamy. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- The pouch already brings fruit and greens, so the Greek yogurt mainly adds protein and a thicker, creamier texture
- The beetroot and ginger juice keeps the smoothie more vegetable-forward and drops the sugar load compared with apple juice
- If you want a thicker breakfast smoothie, cut the juice to 3/4 cup and add a few more ice cubes
- This bumps into 8/10 because it has fruit, greens, fiber, and a little protein without the extra sweetness of a fruit juice base
