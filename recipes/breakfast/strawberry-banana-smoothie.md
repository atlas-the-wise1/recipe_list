# Strawberry Banana Smoothie

**Category:** B — Breakfast
**Tags:** `#heart-healthy` `#low-cholesterol` `#quick`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~110 | ~3g | ~0g | ~24g | ~3g | ~1mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

**Serves:** 3–4
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup coconut water
- ½ cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
- 2 cups fresh strawberries, stemmed
- 1 banana, peeled and cut in half
- 1 cup ice

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Pulse 3 times, then blend on HIGH until smooth. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Protein boost:** Add 1 scoop vanilla whey (+~25g protein, +~120 cal) to turn this into a proper post-workout recovery drink
- **Frozen strawberries** work great and are cheaper — reduce ice by half if using frozen
- **Coconut water** adds natural electrolytes; sub regular water if you don't have it, though you'll lose some sweetness
- **Meal prep:** Pre-bag fruit portions (minus yogurt) and freeze — grab, add yogurt + coconut water, blend
