# Bircher Muesli

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup / 100g old-fashioned rolled oats
- ½ cup / 115g Greek yogurt (preferably full-fat)
- ½ cup / 120ml milk or nondairy milk, plus more as needed
- 2 medium apples, grated on the large holes of a box grater
- ⅓ cup / 40g chopped pecans
- 2 tablespoons tahini *(optional)*
- 2 tablespoons golden raisins
- 2 tablespoons dried cherries
- Fresh fruit, dried fruit, chopped nuts, and maple syrup — for serving

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** In a container with a lid, stir together oats, yogurt, milk, apples, pecans, tahini (if using), raisins, and cherries. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour and up to 4 days.

**STEP 2:** Stir before serving and add a splash of milk if it's gotten too thick. Top individual servings with additional fresh or dried fruit, nuts, and maple syrup.

---

## Notes

- **Ultimate meal-prep breakfast** — make Sunday, eat through Thursday; gets better as oats absorb and soften
- **Grate the apples, don't chop** — grating releases juice into the mix, naturally sweetening it and keeping the texture cohesive
- **Tahini is worth it** — adds richness, healthy fat, and a subtle nuttiness; not optional if you have it
- **Nondairy works great** — oat milk adds creaminess; almond milk keeps it lighter
- **Boost protein:** Add a scoop of vanilla protein powder or swap regular yogurt for higher-protein skyr (~20g protein/serving)
- **Keto-unfriendly** — oats + fruit + raisins = moderate-high carb; not suitable for strict keto
- **Topping ideas:** Sliced banana + honey, fresh berries + pumpkin seeds, or mango + coconut flakes
