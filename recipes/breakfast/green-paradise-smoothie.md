# Green Paradise Smoothie

**Category:** B — Breakfast
**Tags:** `#heart-healthy` `#low-cholesterol` `#quick` `#vegan`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~115 | ~2g | ~0g | ~29g | ~3g | ~0mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

**Serves:** 3–4
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup packed spinach
- 1 cup pineapple chunks
- 1 cup frozen mango chunks
- 1 small banana
- ½ cup ice
- 1½ cups coconut water

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Pulse 3 times, then blend on HIGH until smooth. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Spinach is invisible** — you won't taste it at all; the mango and pineapple completely cover it; great way to sneak in greens
- **Frozen mango** is the base here — keeps it cold and thick without excess ice diluting the flavor
- **Protein boost:** Add 1 scoop vanilla or unflavored whey/plant protein (+~25g protein) — pairs naturally with the tropical flavors
- **Swap spinach for kale** for more nutrients, but blend longer — kale is tougher and can leave stringy bits if underpowered
- **Coconut water** adds electrolytes; sub water if needed — slightly less sweet but still works
