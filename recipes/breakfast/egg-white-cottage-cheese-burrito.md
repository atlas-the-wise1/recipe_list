# Egg White, Cottage Cheese & Turkey Breakfast Burrito

**Category:** B — Breakfast
**Tags:** `#high-protein` `#low-cholesterol` `#fat-loss` `#meal-prep` `#freezer-friendly`
**Servings:** 1 burrito
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 8 min | **Total:** ~18 min

---

**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~390 | ~47g | ~8g | ~28g | ~4g | ~35mg |
**Health Score:** 8/10

---

## Ingredients

- 6 egg whites (or ¾ cup liquid egg whites)
- ½ cup low-fat cottage cheese
- 2 oz lean turkey breast (deli-sliced or crumbled cooked)
- 1 cup fresh spinach
- ½ cup sliced mushrooms
- 1 whole wheat tortilla (large, ~10")
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste
- Optional: hot sauce, salsa, or everything bagel seasoning

---

## Instructions

1. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Spray lightly with olive oil.
2. Sauté mushrooms 3–4 minutes until softened. Add spinach and cook 1 minute until wilted.
3. Add turkey to warm through (1 min).
4. Pour in egg whites; scramble until just set (2–3 min). Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder.
5. Remove from heat. Stir in cottage cheese — residual heat melts it in slightly without drying out.
6. Warm tortilla 20 seconds in microwave or dry skillet.
7. Fill tortilla with egg white mixture, fold sides in, roll tightly.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Freezer method (ET's approach):**
1. Let burritos cool completely before wrapping.
2. Wrap each burrito tightly in foil, then place in a zip-lock freezer bag.
3. Freeze up to **3 months**.
4. **Reheat from frozen:** Remove foil, wrap in damp paper towel, microwave 2–3 min flipping halfway. Or: reheat in foil in oven at 350°F for 15–20 min for crispier tortilla.

**Batch cooking:** Recipe scales cleanly — 12 burritos = ~4.5 dozen egg whites, ~6 cups cottage cheese, 1.5 lbs turkey.

**Storage (fridge):** 4 days — wrap in foil or airtight container.

---

## Notes

- **Cottage cheese tip:** Stir in AFTER eggs are off heat — keeps it creamy, not rubbery.
- **Tortilla swap:** Low-carb wrap saves ~80 cal and ~15g carbs if cutting carbs is priority.
- **Add-ins:** Diced jalapeño, roasted red pepper, or black beans for variety.
- **Salsa option:** Add 2 tbsp fresh salsa for ~10 cal, no cholesterol impact.
