# Emerald Green Smoothie

**Category:** B — Breakfast
**Tags:** `#heart-healthy` `#low-cholesterol` `#quick` `#vegan`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~55 | ~1g | ~0g | ~14g | ~1g | ~0mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

**Serves:** 3–4
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- ½ medium English cucumber, peeled and cut lengthwise
- 1 cup spinach
- 1 cup honeydew melon
- 1 cup cantaloupe
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- ½ cup coconut water
- 1 cup ice

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Pulse 3 times, then blend on HIGH until smooth. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Very low calorie** — this is more of a hydrating refresher than a meal; pair with eggs or a protein source to make it a full breakfast
- **Protein boost:** Add 1 scoop vanilla or unflavored protein powder (+~25g protein) — the light melon flavor is gentle enough not to clash
- **Cucumber:** English cucumber preferred — thinner skin, fewer seeds, blends smoother
- **Lime juice is important** — brightens all the melon flavors; don't skip or sub bottled lime
- **Serve immediately** — melon oxidizes and the color fades fast; not a make-ahead drink
