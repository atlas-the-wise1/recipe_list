# Morning Wake Up Smoothie

**Category:** B — Breakfast
**Tags:** `#heart-healthy` `#low-cholesterol` `#fat-loss` `#quick`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~175 | ~14g | ~2g | ~27g | ~2g | ~8mg |
**Health Score:** 7/10

**Serves:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 small banana, peeled
- ½ cup mango
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 scoop whey protein powder
- 1 cup vanilla almond milk
- ¼ cup fresh orange juice
- 1 cup ice

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Blend on HIGH until smooth. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Layer order matters:** Liquid first (almond milk, OJ), then soft fruit, then protein powder, then ice — helps the blender catch everything without air pockets
- **Frozen mango works great** — skip the extra ice if using frozen, or the smoothie gets too thick
- **Protein powder:** Vanilla whey pairs best; unflavored works if you want to control sweetness
- **Boost fat:** Add ½ tablespoon almond butter for ~50 cal, +2g protein, +4g healthy fat — makes it more satiating
- **Meal prep:** Pre-bag banana + mango portions in the freezer for grab-and-blend mornings
