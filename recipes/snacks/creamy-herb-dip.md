# Creamy Herb Dip

**Category:** S — Snacks / Condiment
**Tags:** `#vegetarian` `#low-cholesterol` `#quick` `#keto` `#high-protein`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~80 | ~5g | ~3g | ~6g | ~0g | ~13mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

**Serves:** 2–4
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup low-fat sour cream
- ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon fresh dill
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 1 scallion, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon milk

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Blend on MEDIUM until smooth.

**STEP 3:** Transfer to a small serving bowl. Serve with veggies, pita, or crackers.

---

## Notes

- **Greek yogurt** replaces half the sour cream — adds protein and cuts fat without losing creaminess
- **Fresh herbs only** — dried dill and dried parsley will give you a muddy, flat flavor; fresh is essential here
- **Make ahead:** Flavors deepen after 1–2 hours in the fridge — make it before guests arrive; keeps 5 days refrigerated
- **Serve with:** Cucumber slices, bell pepper strips, carrots, pita chips — works as a veggie dip, spread, or sauce on grilled chicken
- **Full-fat swap:** Use full-fat sour cream for a richer version — adds ~40 cal and 4g fat per serving
