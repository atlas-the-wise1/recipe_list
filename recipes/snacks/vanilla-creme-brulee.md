# Vanilla Crème Brûlée

**Category:** S — Snack
**Tags:** `#dessert`
**Serves:** 4
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 40 min | **Total:** ~55 min + chilling

## Ingredients
- 2 cups cream
- 1 vanilla bean or 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 5 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup sugar, plus more for topping

## Instructions
1. Heat oven to 325°F. Warm cream, vanilla, and salt; let sit.
2. Whisk yolks and sugar. Temper with cream, then pour into 4 ramekins in a baking dish with hot water halfway up the sides.
3. Bake 30 to 40 minutes until barely set. Chill.
4. Top with sugar and broil until browned, about 5 minutes.
