# Watermelon Mint Granita

---

## Ingredients

- 6 cups watermelon, peeled, seeded, and roughly cut
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 3 tablespoons agave
- 6–8 fresh mint leaves, chopped

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher and blend on HIGH for 45 seconds until smooth. Strain the watermelon juice and discard the pulp.

**STEP 2:** Pour into a shallow bowl or ice cube trays. Freeze 3–4 hours until almost solid.

**STEP 3:** Using a fork, scrape and shave the frozen mixture into icy flakes. Spoon into glasses and serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Shallow container = better granita** — more surface area means faster, more even freezing and easier shaving
- **Scrape every hour** for a fluffier, more granular texture — or just freeze solid and scrape all at once for chunkier shards
- **Agave:** Reduce to 2 tbsp if your watermelon is very ripe and sweet — taste the juice before adding
- **Make ahead:** Freeze solid, then scrape right before serving — keeps up to 1 week in the freezer
- **Summer crowd-pleaser:** Scale up easily — watermelon is cheap in season and this impresses with zero effort
