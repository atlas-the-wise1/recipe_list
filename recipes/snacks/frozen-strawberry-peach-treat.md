# Frozen Strawberry Peach Treat

**Category:** S — Snacks
**Tags:** `#vegetarian` `#low-cholesterol` `#heart-healthy` `#fat-loss` `#quick`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~87 | ~2g | ~1g | ~17g | ~1g | ~3mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

**Serves:** 4
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1½ cups frozen strawberries
- ½ cup frozen peaches
- ½ cup whole milk
- ¼ cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
- 2 tablespoons agave
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Blend on HIGH until ingredients thicken into a soft-serve consistency.

**STEP 3:** Garnish with your favorite fruit toppings and serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Consistency:** This lands between a thick smoothie and soft-serve — serve immediately; it melts fast
- **Topping ideas:** Sliced fresh strawberries, granola, shredded coconut, or a drizzle of honey
- **Dairy swap:** Sub almond milk for whole milk and coconut yogurt for a fully vegan version — same texture
- **Only 87 cal** — one of the lightest desserts in the library; doubles easily without guilt
- **Agave:** Reduce to 1 tbsp if your frozen fruit is already sweet
