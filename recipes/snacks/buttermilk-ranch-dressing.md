# Buttermilk Ranch Dressing

**Category:** S — Snacks / Condiment
**Tags:** `#vegetarian` `#quick` `#keto` `#meal-prep`
**Nutrition (per 2 tablespoon serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~63 | ~0g | ~7g | ~0g | ~0g | ~5mg |
**Makes:** ~3 cups (24 servings)
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Chill Time:** 30 min | **Total:** ~35 min

---

**Health Score:** 4/10

## Ingredients

- ½ cup buttermilk
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ¼ cup fresh parsley leaves
- 12 sprigs fresh chive, cut into 1-inch batons
- ⅛ cup fresh tarragon
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Blend on MEDIUM until smooth. Chill before serving.

---

## Notes

- **Chill before serving** — flavors meld and the dressing thickens as it colds; 30 min minimum, overnight is better
- **Fresh herbs are non-negotiable** — the trio of parsley, chive, and tarragon is what separates this from bottled ranch
- **Thinner dressing:** Add more buttermilk 1 tbsp at a time until desired consistency; thicker = dip, thinner = dressing
- **Fridge life:** Keeps up to 2 weeks refrigerated — make a full batch Sunday, use all week
- **Lighten it up:** Sub half the mayo for plain Greek yogurt — cuts ~30 cal and 3g fat per serving while keeping it creamy
