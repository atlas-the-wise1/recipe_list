# Lite Mint Cookies & Cream Ice Cream

**Category:** S — Snacks
**Tags:** `#vegan` `#low-cholesterol` `#dairy-free` `#nut-free`
**⚠️ Requires:** Ninja CREAMi machine
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~158 | ~1g | ~10g | ~17g | ~0g | ~0mg |
**Health Score:** 4/10

**Serves:** 4
**Prep Time:** 5–10 min | **Freeze Time:** 24 hours | **Best Within:** 2 weeks

---

## Ingredients

- ¾ cup unsweetened coconut cream
- ¼ cup monk fruit sweetener with erythritol
- 2 tablespoons raw agave nectar
- 5–6 drops green food coloring
- ½ teaspoon mint extract
- 1 cup unsweetened oat milk
- 3 chocolate sandwich cookies, cut into quarters *(for mix-in)*

---

## Tools Needed
- Large bowl + whisk
- Ninja CREAMi pint container with lid

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** In a large bowl, whisk unsweetened coconut cream until smooth. Add monk fruit sweetener, agave nectar, green food coloring, and mint extract. Whisk until well combined and sweetener is fully dissolved. Add oat milk and whisk to combine.

**STEP 2:** Pour base into an empty CREAMi pint container. Place storage lid on pint and freeze for 24 hours.

**STEP 3:** Remove pint from freezer and remove lid. Process on the **LITE ICE CREAM** function.

**STEP 4:** Using a spoon, create a 1½-inch wide hole reaching the bottom of the pint. Add quartered cookie pieces into the hole and process again using the **MIX-IN** program.

**STEP 5:** When processing is complete, remove ice cream from pint and serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **LITE ICE CREAM function** — not regular Ice Cream; use the correct program or texture will be off
- **MIX-IN program is step 2** — don't add cookies before the first process or they'll get pulverized; add after first spin for chunky cookie pieces
- **Mint extract:** Start with ¼ tsp if you're sensitive to mint — it's potent; ½ tsp is strong and refreshing
- **Food coloring is optional** — purely visual; the ice cream tastes the same without it
- **Cookie swap:** Sub any chocolate sandwich cookie — store-brand works fine; Oreos are technically vegan
- **24-hour freeze is mandatory** — same rule as all CREAMi recipes; don't shortcut
- **Re-spin tip:** If texture is crumbly, add 1 tbsp oat milk and re-spin before adding mix-ins
