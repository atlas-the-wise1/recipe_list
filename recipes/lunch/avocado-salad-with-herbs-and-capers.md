# Avocado Salad With Herbs and Capers

**Category:** Lunch
**Tags:** #vegetarian #gluten-free #quick #heart-healthy
**Servings:** 4
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** ~10 min

## Ingredients

- 1 large bunch cilantro
- 1 large bunch parsley
- 2 scallions, very finely chopped
- 1 jalapeño, seeded and very finely chopped
- 1 to 2 garlic cloves, finely grated or minced
- 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt, more to taste
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, more to taste
- 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil, more for serving
- 4 Hass avocados, peeled, pitted, and sliced
- 4 teaspoons capers, drained
- Flaky sea salt and black pepper

## Instructions

1. Reserve 1 cup of whole cilantro leaves and tender stems. Finely chop the rest and transfer to a medium bowl.
2. Reserve 1 cup of whole parsley leaves and tender stems. Finely chop the rest and add to the bowl with the cilantro. Add the scallions, jalapeño, garlic, fine sea salt, and red wine vinegar. Stir in the olive oil. Taste and add more salt, vinegar, or both if needed.
3. Scatter the whole cilantro and parsley leaves over 4 serving plates. Fan the avocado slices on top and sprinkle lightly with fine salt. Spoon the herb dressing over the avocado, making sure to include the oil in the bowl, then top with capers. Finish with flaky sea salt and black pepper.

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** Best fresh, but the herb dressing keeps 1 day refrigerated  
**Reheat:** Not applicable  
**Batch size:** Easy to scale for a larger salad platter, but slice the avocados last  
**Notes:** This is bright, salty, and herb-heavy, with the capers and jalapeño giving the avocado enough punch to feel like a full dish. The olive oil and avocado make it rich, so a little goes a long way.
