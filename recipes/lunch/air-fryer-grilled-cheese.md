## Ingredients

- 2 slices sandwich bread, each no more than 1/2-inch thick
- Mayonnaise, as needed
- 2 to 4 slices melting cheese (such as sharp Cheddar, American, or pepper Jack), depending on thickness of cheese

---

## Instructions

1. Heat air fryer to 400 degrees, if preheating is recommended for your air fryer.
2. Thinly spread one side of each bread slice with mayonnaise. Place one slice of bread, mayonnaise side down, in the air fryer basket and top with the desired amount of cheese. Top with the second slice of bread, mayonnaise side up. Cook until the sandwich is golden and crusty all over and the cheese is melted, about 3 minutes per side.

---

## Notes

- The mayo helps the bread brown evenly in the air fryer
- Use sturdy bread so the sandwich holds together while cooking
- Check a little early if your air fryer runs hot
