# Double Turkey Burger on Brioche

---

## Ingredients

- 2 turkey burger patties
- 1 brioche bun, split and toasted
- 1 tablespoon ketchup
- 1 cup shredded lettuce

---

## Instructions

1. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Season the patties with salt and pepper if needed.
2. Cook the patties for 4 to 5 minutes per side, until browned and cooked through to 165 degrees F. If the patties are already cooked, rewarm them until hot instead of cooking them from raw.
3. Split the brioche bun and toast it lightly so it holds up to the burger juices.
4. Spread the ketchup on the bottom bun, add the lettuce, stack on both patties, and finish with the top bun.
5. Serve immediately while the bun is warm and the patties are juicy.

---

## Notes

- This is built for a quick lunch using two turkey patties and a bakery brioche bun.
- If you want it a little sharper, add mustard or pickles; if you want it cleaner, keep it to ketchup and lettuce only.
- For meal prep, store the patties separately from the bun and lettuce so the sandwich stays intact when reheated.
