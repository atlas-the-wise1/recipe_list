# Endive, Fennel & Radish Salad

**Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

- 2 Belgian endives, leaves separated
- 4–5 radishes, thinly sliced (variety of colors if available)
- ½ small fennel bulb, cored and thinly sliced (~½ cup)
- 2 tbsp good-quality white-wine vinegar
- ½ tsp orange zest
- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp olive oil
- ¼ cup Italian parsley leaves, torn
- 1 tbsp chives, cut on an angle into ½-inch pieces
- 1 tbsp roughly chopped tarragon
- Salt and pepper, to taste

---

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, gently toss endive leaves, radishes, and fennel.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together white-wine vinegar, orange zest, olive oil, parsley, chives, and tarragon.
3. Season dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
4. Spoon dressing over salad just before serving.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** Salad components (undressed) 2 days fridge. Dressing 5 days fridge in a sealed jar.
**Reheat:** N/A — serve cold or room temperature.
**Batch size:** Doubles easily — great for meal prepping lunches for the week.
**Pairs well with:** Air-Fryer Salmon, Blue-Cheese Steak, grilled chicken, or any lean protein.
**Notes:** Zero cholesterol, high fiber, heart-healthy fats from olive oil. Excellent weekly staple side. Dress just before eating to keep endive crisp.
