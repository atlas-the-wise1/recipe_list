# Sheet-Pan Tofu and Brussels Sprouts With Hoisin-Tahini Sauce

**Category:** L — Lunch

**Serves:** 4
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 30 min | **Total:** ~40 min

---

## Ingredients

### Brussels Sprouts and Tofu
- 2 pounds brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
- Extra-virgin olive oil
- Kosher salt (such as Diamond Crystal) and black pepper
- 2 (14-ounce) packages extra-firm tofu, drained and cut into ¼-inch thick slices

### Hoisin-Tahini Sauce
- 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce
- 3 tablespoons tahini
- 1 garlic clove, grated

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Heat oven to 450°F and arrange racks in the bottom and middle positions.

**STEP 2:** Place brussels sprouts on one rimmed sheet pan. Drizzle with about 2 tablespoons olive oil, season with 1 teaspoon salt and a few turns of black pepper. Toss to coat well.

**STEP 3:** On the second sheet pan, drizzle 1–2 tablespoons olive oil and tilt to coat the surface. Lay tofu slices on the pan and season with 1–1½ teaspoons salt and several turns of black pepper. Drizzle tops with more olive oil.

**STEP 4:** Roast both pans for 25–30 minutes, switching racks halfway through. Brussels sprouts should be tender and crispy in spots; tofu should be lightly golden.

**STEP 5:** Meanwhile, whisk together hoisin, tahini, garlic, and 3 tablespoons water until combined. Sauce should be thick but pourable — add a splash more water if needed.

**STEP 6:** Serve brussels sprouts alongside tofu and drizzle generously with hoisin-tahini sauce. Eat warm or at room temperature.

---

## Notes

- **Tofu texture:** Pressing the tofu for 15–20 min before slicing removes more moisture and gets a crispier result — worth it if you have time
- **Meal prep friendly:** Keeps well refrigerated 4 days; sauce stores separately; reheat in air fryer or 400°F oven for 5 min to re-crisp
- **Swap:** Sub broccolini or cauliflower for brussels sprouts if preferred — same cook time
- **Boost calories:** Serve over brown rice or quinoa (+200 cal, +5g protein) to make it a more substantial meal
- **Hoisin note:** Hoisin adds sugar — if strictly keto, swap for coconut aminos + a drop of sesame oil
