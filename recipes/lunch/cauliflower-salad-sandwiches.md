# Cauliflower Salad Sandwiches

**Category:** L — Lunch
**Tags:** `#heart-healthy` `#low-cholesterol` `#meal-prep` `#vegetarian`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 580kcal |
| Protein | 14g |
| Carbs | 62g |
| Fat | 32g |
| Fiber | 6g |
| Cholesterol | 25mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** ~40 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 large head cauliflower (about 2 lbs), cored, halved, and cut into 1-inch slices
- 6 tablespoons neutral oil
- 5 teaspoons curry powder
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 1 cup / 4 oz raw walnuts, roughly chopped
- 1 tart, crisp apple, cored and chopped into ½-inch pieces
- 1 lemon, zested and juiced, plus more to taste
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup strained yogurt (Greek or skyr)
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup cilantro leaves and tender stems, roughly chopped
- ½ cup parsley leaves, roughly chopped
- 4 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place a large rimmed baking sheet on the middle rack of the oven and heat to 450°F.

**STEP 2:** In a large bowl, toss cauliflower with 3 tablespoons oil, 2 teaspoons curry powder, and a large pinch of salt. Massage to separate into individual florets and coat evenly. Carefully spread onto the hot baking sheet. Roast until cooked through and charred in spots, about 20 minutes. Turn broiler on high and cook until deep brown and charred, 3–5 minutes.

**STEP 3:** While cauliflower roasts, toast the walnuts: In a small skillet over medium heat, add remaining 3 tablespoons oil, walnuts, and a pinch of salt. Cook, stirring frequently, until lightly toasted — 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in remaining 3 teaspoons curry powder, turn off heat, and cook until fragrant — about 1 minute.

**STEP 4:** In a large bowl, stir together apple, lemon zest and juice, and a large pinch of salt. Add mayonnaise, yogurt, raisins, cilantro, parsley, and the walnut mixture (scrape in all the oil and curry). Stir to combine.

**STEP 5:** Add cauliflower and toss until well combined. Season with salt to taste. Cauliflower salad keeps in the fridge for up to 3 days.

**STEP 6:** Divide cauliflower salad among rolls and press firmly to close. Eat immediately or up to 12 hours later; refrigerate if not eating within the hour.

---

## Notes

- **Great meal prep:** Cauliflower salad lasts 3 days in the fridge — make the filling Sunday, assemble sandwiches through Wednesday
- **Lighter swap:** Sub half the mayo for more Greek yogurt to cut ~80 cal/sandwich and reduce saturated fat
- **Skip the roll:** Serve over arugula or butter lettuce as a grain-free bowl — saves ~150 cal and makes it keto-friendlier
- **Protein boost:** Add a sliced hard-boiled egg or rotisserie chicken to each sandwich (~8–15g more protein)
- **Apple choice:** Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work best — need the tartness to balance the curry and raisins
- **Hot baking sheet trick:** Preheating the pan is what creates the initial sear and char — don't skip it
