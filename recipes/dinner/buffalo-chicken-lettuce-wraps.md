# Buffalo Chicken Lettuce Wraps

**Category:** D — Dinner
**Tags:** `#keto` `#high-protein` `#low-cholesterol` `#fat-loss`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~320 | ~35g | ~15g | ~6g | ~1g | ~70mg |
**Health Score:** 8/10

**Serves:** 4 (2 wraps per person)
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 12 min | **Total:** ~22 min

---

## Ingredients

**For the chicken:**
- 1.25 lbs ground chicken (93/7 lean)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- ¾ cup buffalo sauce (Frank's RedHot or similar)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ tsp onion powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

**For serving:**
- 8–10 large butter lettuce leaves (or romaine/iceberg as substitute)
- 2 cups celery, cut into sticks
- 2 cups shredded carrots
- ½ cup crumbled blue cheese (or 6 tbsp ranch dressing)
- Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish (optional)

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
**STEP 2:** Add ground chicken, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon as it cooks. Stir frequently until browned and cooked through, about 8–10 minutes.
**STEP 3:** Add minced garlic and onion powder. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant, stirring constantly.
**STEP 4:** Pour buffalo sauce into the skillet and stir until chicken is evenly coated. Simmer for 1–2 minutes to warm through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
**STEP 5:** Gently separate butter lettuce leaves and arrange on a serving platter. Pat dry with paper towels to prevent sogginess.
**STEP 6:** Spoon warm buffalo chicken into each lettuce leaf, leaving room at the base for wrapping.
**STEP 7:** Add a small handful of celery and carrots to each wrap. Drizzle with blue cheese crumbles or ranch dressing.
**STEP 8:** Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Rotisserie swap:** Use shredded rotisserie chicken (about 3 cups) instead of ground chicken; skip browning and simply heat through with buffalo sauce (5 minutes total).
- **Keto-compliant:** This is naturally low-carb—no bread, no sugar-heavy sauce. Use sugar-free buffalo sauce if available for even leaner macro profile.
- **Make-ahead strategy:** Cook buffalo chicken up to 3 days ahead and store in the fridge. Assemble wraps fresh at mealtime to prevent lettuce from wilting.
- **Sauce heat level:** Adjust spice by using half buffalo sauce and half plain hot sauce, or add a splash of ranch to cool it down if needed.
- **Blue cheese alternative:** Swap for crumbled goat cheese, feta, or even avocado for a creamier texture with fewer calories.
- **Meal prep format:** Portion cooked chicken into containers with celery and carrots on the side; guests assemble their own wraps.
- **Temperature control:** Don't let the skillet exceed medium-high or the chicken will dry out; ground poultry cooks quickly.
