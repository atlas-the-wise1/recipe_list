# Sheet-Pan Chicken With Artichokes and Herbs

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** #heart-healthy #high-protein #low-cholesterol #fat-loss #keto
**Servings:** 4 (2 thighs each)
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 40 min | **Total:** ~55 min

---

**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~520 | ~48g | ~30g | ~18g | ~7g | ~140mg |
**Health Score:** 8/10

---

## Ingredients

- 3 large fennel bulbs (~3 lbs), trimmed, halved lengthwise and cut into ½-inch-thick wedges
- 2 (14 oz) cans whole artichoke hearts in water, drained and halved lengthwise
- 8 oz cherry tomatoes
- 6 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 2 tbsp minced garlic
- 2 tbsp ground fennel
- 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh rosemary
- 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh sage
- 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (~3 lbs)
- ½ cup pitted green olives, chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 tsp red-pepper flakes (optional)

---

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to **425°F**. On a rimmed baking sheet, combine fennel wedges, artichokes, and tomatoes with 3 tbsp olive oil. Season with salt and pepper and toss to coat evenly. Spread in an even layer.
2. In a small bowl, combine garlic, ground fennel, rosemary, sage, and remaining 3 tbsp olive oil. Season chicken all over with salt and pepper, then rub with the spice mixture. Arrange chicken on top of the vegetables skin-side up.
3. Roast until vegetables are tender and chicken is golden, crisp, and cooked through, **35–40 minutes**.
4. Divide chicken and vegetables among plates. Pour pan juices into a small bowl, let settle, then skim off fat with a spoon. Stir in olives, lemon juice, and red-pepper flakes (if using). Spoon sauce over chicken and vegetables. Serve warm.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** 4 days fridge / 2 months freezer
**Reheat:** Oven at 375°F for 10–12 min to re-crisp the skin. Microwave works for the vegetables.
**Batch size:** Recipe makes 8 thighs — halve for 2 servings or keep full batch for the week
**Notes:** Deeply flavored from the fennel-herb rub and the artichoke-tomato base. The pan sauce with olives and lemon is the finishing touch — don't skip the fat-skimming step, it lightens it up considerably. Fennel and artichokes are excellent for heart health and digestion. One of the more impressive sheet-pan dinners in the book with minimal hands-on effort.
