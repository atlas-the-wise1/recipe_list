# Chicken Milanese

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** #high-protein
**Servings:** 4
**Prep Time:** 20 min | **Cook Time:** 15 min | **Total:** ~35 min

---

**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~580 | ~52g | ~28g | ~28g | ~2g | ~175mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (~10 oz each)
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 large eggs, beaten
- 1½ cups panko bread crumbs
- ½ cup finely grated Parmesan
- ~1 cup olive oil (or neutral oil), for pan-frying

### Salad
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice, plus lemon wedges for serving
- 5 oz arugula (~5 packed cups)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes (optional), halved
- ½ cup shaved Parmesan

---

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together onion powder, garlic powder, 2 tsp salt, and 1½ tsp pepper. Set aside.
2. Set each chicken breast on a cutting board and slice in half horizontally. Pound each piece to no more than ½-inch thick using a meat mallet or heavy skillet bottom. Season generously on both sides with half the seasoned salt.
3. Set up a breading station: flour in one shallow bowl, beaten eggs in a second, panko + grated Parmesan + remaining seasoned salt in a third. Dip each piece in flour → egg → panko, pressing panko in firmly to fully coat. Transfer to a baking sheet. *(Can refrigerate breaded chicken for a few hours at this point.)*
4. In a medium nonstick skillet, heat a ½-inch layer of oil over medium-high. Working in two batches, cook chicken undisturbed until golden on both sides, **~3 min per side**. Transfer to a wire rack and lightly sprinkle with salt if desired.
5. **Salad:** Whisk olive oil and lemon juice in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Add arugula, cherry tomatoes, and shaved Parmesan. Toss to coat and season again.
6. Divide chicken among plates, pile salad alongside. Serve immediately with lemon wedges.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** Cooked chicken 3 days fridge / 2 months freezer. Breaded uncooked chicken holds in fridge for a few hours.
**Reheat:** Oven at 375°F on a wire rack for 10–12 min to re-crisp. Avoid microwave — kills the crust.
**Batch size:** Recipe makes 4 cutlets — doubles easily. Bread all at once and fry in batches.
**Notes:** Protein powerhouse at 52g per serving. Higher in calories due to pan-frying oil absorption and Parmesan — air-frying the cutlets instead of pan-frying cuts fat significantly (~380 cal, ~15g fat) and keeps the crust crispy. Worth considering as a swap for weekly rotation. Best served fresh for optimal crust texture. See `chicken-milanese-air-fryer.md` for the healthier air-fryer variant.
