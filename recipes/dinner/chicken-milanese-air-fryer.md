# Chicken Milanese (Air-Fryer Version)

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** #high-protein #heart-healthy #low-cholesterol #fat-loss
**Servings:** 4
**Prep Time:** 20 min | **Cook Time:** 14 min | **Total:** ~34 min

---

**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~380 | ~52g | ~10g | ~28g | ~2g | ~155mg |

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (~10 oz each)
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 large eggs, beaten
- 1½ cups panko bread crumbs
- ½ cup finely grated Parmesan
- Olive oil spray

### Salad
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice, plus lemon wedges for serving
- 5 oz arugula (~5 packed cups)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes (optional), halved
- ½ cup shaved Parmesan

---

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together onion powder, garlic powder, 2 tsp salt, and 1½ tsp pepper. Set aside.
2. Set each chicken breast on a cutting board and slice in half horizontally. Pound each piece to no more than ½-inch thick using a meat mallet or heavy skillet bottom. Season generously on both sides with half the seasoned salt.
3. Set up a breading station: flour in one shallow bowl, beaten eggs in a second, panko + grated Parmesan + remaining seasoned salt in a third. Dip each piece in flour → egg → panko, pressing panko in firmly to fully coat. Transfer to a plate.
4. Heat air fryer to 400°F. Spray the air fryer basket with olive oil spray. Place breaded cutlets in a single layer (cook in batches if needed — do not overlap). Spray tops generously with olive oil spray.
5. Cook for **7 minutes**, flip, spray tops again with olive oil spray, and cook another **5–7 minutes** until golden, crispy, and internal temp reaches **165°F**.
6. Transfer to a wire rack and lightly sprinkle with salt if desired.
7. **Salad:** Whisk olive oil and lemon juice in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Add arugula, cherry tomatoes, and shaved Parmesan. Toss to coat and season again.
8. Divide chicken among plates, pile salad alongside. Serve immediately with lemon wedges.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** Cooked chicken 3 days fridge / 2 months freezer.
**Reheat:** Air fryer at 375°F for 4–5 min — crust comes back almost like fresh. Best reheating method by far.
**Batch size:** Bread all cutlets at once, air-fry in batches of 2. Takes ~30 min total for 4 cutlets.
**vs. Pan-Fried version:** 200 fewer calories, 18g less fat per serving — same protein, same crust texture. Strong weekly rotation candidate.
**Notes:** The olive oil spray is key — don't skip it or the panko won't crisp properly. Two light coats (before + after flip) is all you need.
