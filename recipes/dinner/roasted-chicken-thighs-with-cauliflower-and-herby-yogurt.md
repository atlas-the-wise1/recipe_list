# Roasted Chicken Thighs With Cauliflower and Herby Yogurt

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** `#high-protein` `#heart-healthy` `#meal-prep` `#fat-loss`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~430 | ~31g | ~28g | ~12g | ~3g | ~150mg |
**Health Score:** 7/10
**Serves:** 6
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 40 min | **Total:** ~55 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon Aleppo pepper flakes, or 1/2 teaspoon red-pepper flakes
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs, about 3 pounds
- 1 small head cauliflower, cut into 2-inch florets, about 5 cups
- 3 shallots, quartered lengthwise
- 1 1/4 cups Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, plus more for garnish
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice, plus more for serving
- 1 garlic clove, finely grated

---

## Instructions

1. Heat the oven to 425 F and set a rack in the center.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the coriander, paprika, Aleppo pepper, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and a big pinch of salt and pepper. Add the chicken and toss to coat. Arrange the chicken, skin-side up, on a large baking sheet.
3. Add the cauliflower, shallots, remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper to the bowl. Toss to coat, then arrange the vegetables in a single layer around the chicken.
4. Roast until the chicken is deep golden and cooked through and the cauliflower is browned, tossing the vegetables once or twice, about 40 minutes. If the chicken finishes first, move it to a plate, cover, and continue roasting the cauliflower until browned.
5. While the chicken roasts, whisk together the yogurt, mint, cilantro, lemon juice, garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate until serving.
6. Transfer the chicken and vegetables to a platter, spoon over any sheet-pan juices, and serve with the herby yogurt and more lemon if you want brightness.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

- **Storage:** 3 days fridge
- **Reheat:** Warm in a 350 F oven or low skillet so the skin and vegetables hold texture
- **Batch size:** Scale to 6 servings for a full dinner with leftovers
