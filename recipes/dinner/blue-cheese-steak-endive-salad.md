# Blue-Cheese Steak and Endive Salad for Two

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** #high-protein #keto
**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 10 min | **Total:** ~25 min

---

## Macros (per serving, estimated)
| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~620 | ~48g | ~8g | ~44g | ~3g | ~165mg |

---

## Ingredients

### Steak
- 4 tbsp unsalted butter, slightly softened
- 2 tbsp good-quality blue cheese (Roquefort, Stilton, or Gorgonzola)
- 1 tbsp finely chopped shallot
- ¾ to 1 lb hanger or skirt steak, at room temperature
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 1 tbsp canola or grapeseed oil
- Flaky salt (optional)

### Salad
- 2 Belgian endives, leaves separated
- 4–5 radishes, thinly sliced (variety of colors if available)
- ½ small fennel bulb, cored and thinly sliced (~½ cup)
- 2 tbsp good-quality white-wine vinegar
- ½ tsp orange zest
- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp olive oil
- ¼ cup Italian parsley leaves, torn
- 1 tbsp chives, cut on an angle into ½-inch pieces
- 1 tbsp roughly chopped tarragon

---

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together butter, blue cheese, and shallot. Set aside.
2. Season steak well with salt and pepper on both sides. Set aside at room temperature.
3. **Assemble the salad:** In a large bowl, gently toss endive leaves, radishes, and fennel. In a small bowl, whisk together white-wine vinegar, orange zest, olive oil, parsley, chives, and tarragon. Season with salt and pepper. Set dressing aside.
4. Heat a large cast-iron pan over medium-high heat until very hot, 2–3 minutes. Add canola oil. When it shimmers, add steak and cook undisturbed until crisped and browned on one side, **3–4 minutes**. Flip and cook ~3 more minutes until internal temp reads **130°F** for medium-rare.
5. Move steak to a cutting board. Spread ~2 tbsp blue cheese butter over top and let rest **5–7 minutes**.
6. Slice steak against the grain. Finish with flaky salt if desired.
7. Plate steak and serve salad alongside, spooning dressing over the salad.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** Steak 3 days fridge / 2 months freezer (uncooked). Salad components best fresh — dress day-of.
**Reheat:** Slice cold steak thin and serve over fresh salad — excellent next-day lunch. Or reheat in cast iron 1–2 min per side.
**Batch size:** Double the steak for 4 servings — blue cheese butter scales easily and keeps in fridge 1 week.
**Notes:** High protein and keto-friendly. Higher in fat (~18g saturated fat) and cholesterol (165mg) due to butter + blue cheese — treat as an occasional dinner rather than a weekly staple. The salad component (endive, fennel, radish) is excellent for heart health and can be made standalone. See `blue-cheese-steak-endive-salad-healthier.md` for a version using herb-yogurt topping and olive oil that earns #heart-healthy.
