# Sheet-Pan Chicken With Jammy Tomatoes and Pancetta

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** `#high-protein` `#weeknight` `#sheet-pan`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~540 | ~36g | ~33g | ~12g | ~2g | ~145mg |
**Health Score:** 6/10

**Serves:** 4
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 35 min | **Total:** ~50 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more as needed
- 9 whole unpeeled garlic cloves
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
- 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, plus more for serving
- 1 tablespoon sweet or smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar, optional
- 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2 ounces diced pancetta or bacon
- 1/4 cup torn fresh dill, parsley, or other herbs, for serving
- Freshly ground black pepper

---

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 F. Season the chicken all over with salt and arrange on a rimmed baking sheet.
2. Smash the garlic cloves with the side of a knife and peel them. Finely grate 1 garlic clove into a small bowl, then stir in 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice, paprika, oregano, brown sugar if using, and cumin. Pour over the chicken and toss to coat.
3. Add the tomatoes and the remaining smashed garlic cloves to the baking sheet. Season the tomatoes lightly with salt, drizzle with a little more olive oil, and scatter the pancetta on top.
4. Roast until the chicken is golden and cooked through, about 30 to 35 minutes. After 15 minutes, stir the tomatoes and pancetta, but do not disturb the chicken.
5. Transfer the chicken to plates. Stir the tomatoes and pancetta around in the pan, scraping up the browned bits, then stir in the herbs and black pepper to taste. Add salt if needed and a squeeze of lemon juice, then spoon the tomatoes, garlic, and pancetta over the chicken.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

- Storage: 3 to 4 days refrigerated
- Reheat: Warm gently in a 375 F oven or in a skillet over low heat
- Batch size: Good for a full dinner or meal-prep chicken for the week

---

## Notes

- The jammy tomatoes and pancetta make the pan juices the best part.
- This is richer than a plain roasted chicken tray, but still very workable for a weeknight.
- If you want it lighter later, cut back slightly on the pancetta and olive oil.
