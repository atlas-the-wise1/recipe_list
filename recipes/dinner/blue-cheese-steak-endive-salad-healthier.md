# Blue-Cheese Steak and Endive Salad for Two — Healthier Variant

> 💡 **Healthier Variant** of Blue-Cheese Steak and Endive Salad for Two — see `blue-cheese-steak-endive-salad.md` for the classic version.

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** #high-protein #keto #heart-healthy #low-cholesterol
**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 10 min | **Total:** ~25 min

---

**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~430 | ~48g | ~22g | ~8g | ~3g | ~95mg |

**What changed vs. classic:** ~190 fewer calories, ~22g less fat, ~70mg less cholesterol per serving. Achieves this by:
- Replacing **4 tbsp butter + 2 tbsp blue cheese** topping with a **Greek yogurt–herb sauce** (same tangy richness, a fraction of the sat fat)
- Using **extra-lean hanger or flank steak** trimmed of visible fat instead of untrimmed hanger
- Cooking with **1 tsp olive oil** instead of canola oil + butter
- Keeping the full endive/fennel/radish salad — this component was already outstanding

---

## Ingredients

### Steak
- ¾ to 1 lb hanger or flank steak, well-trimmed of fat, at room temperature
- Kosher salt and black pepper
- 1 tsp olive oil

### Herb-Yogurt Topping (replaces blue cheese butter)
- ¼ cup full-fat Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp finely crumbled blue cheese (use just enough for flavor — ~½ oz)
- 1 tbsp finely chopped shallot
- 1 tbsp fresh chives or parsley, minced
- ½ tsp lemon juice
- Pinch of salt

### Salad (unchanged)
- 2 Belgian endives, leaves separated
- 4–5 radishes, thinly sliced
- ½ small fennel bulb, cored and thinly sliced (~½ cup)
- 2 tbsp white-wine vinegar
- ½ tsp orange zest
- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp olive oil
- ¼ cup Italian parsley leaves, torn
- 1 tbsp chives, cut on an angle into ½-inch pieces
- 1 tbsp roughly chopped tarragon
- Salt and pepper, to taste

---

## Instructions

1. **Make the herb-yogurt topping:** Stir together Greek yogurt, blue cheese, shallot, chives, lemon juice, and salt. Refrigerate until serving. (Can make 1 day ahead.)
2. **Assemble the salad:** In a large bowl, gently toss endive leaves, radishes, and fennel. Whisk together white-wine vinegar, orange zest, olive oil, parsley, chives, and tarragon. Season with salt and pepper. Set dressing aside.
3. Season steak well with salt and pepper on both sides.
4. Heat a large cast-iron pan over medium-high heat until very hot, 2–3 minutes. Add olive oil. When it shimmers, add steak and cook undisturbed until crisped and browned on one side, **3–4 minutes**. Flip and cook ~3 more minutes until internal temp reads **130°F** for medium-rare.
5. Move steak to a cutting board. Spoon ~2 tbsp herb-yogurt topping over top. Let rest **5–7 minutes**.
6. Slice steak against the grain. Spoon dressing over salad. Plate steak alongside salad with a generous dollop of remaining herb-yogurt topping.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** Steak 3 days fridge. Herb-yogurt topping 3 days fridge. Salad components best fresh — dress day-of.
**Notes:** The Greek yogurt topping reads as rich and indulgent but has a fraction of the saturated fat of compound butter. The 1 tbsp blue cheese gives enough punchy flavor without the cholesterol hit of 2 full tablespoons. A near-perfect keto and fat-loss dinner — high satiety from protein, minimal carbs, heart-healthy fats.
