# Turmeric-Black Pepper Chicken With Asparagus

**Serves:** 4
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 10 min | **Total:** ~20 min

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons honey
- 3/4 teaspoon black pepper, plus more to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground turmeric
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 tablespoon coconut or canola oil
- 12 ounces asparagus, trimmed and thinly sliced on an angle
- 1 teaspoon unseasoned rice vinegar or soy sauce
- 1 lime, cut into wedges (optional)

## Instructions

1. Stir together 1/4 cup water, honey, pepper, and salt; set aside.
2. Toss chicken with flour, turmeric, and 1 teaspoon salt.
3. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet and cook chicken until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
4. Add asparagus, season lightly, and cook until crisp-tender, 1 to 2 minutes.
5. Add the honey mixture and cook until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens, 2 to 3 minutes.
6. Remove from heat and stir in vinegar. Serve with lime if you like.
