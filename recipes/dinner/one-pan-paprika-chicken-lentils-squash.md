# One-Pan Paprika Chicken With Lentils, Squash and Daqa

**Category:** D — Dinner
**Tags:** `#heart-healthy` `#high-protein` `#meal-prep`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~580 | ~48g | ~26g | ~38g | ~10g | ~145mg |
**Health Score:** 8/10

**Serves:** 4–6
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** ~70 min | **Total:** ~85 min

---

## Ingredients

- 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (about 2¼ lbs)
- ¾ lb skin-on kabocha squash, seeds removed, cut into ~6 (1-inch-thick) wedges
- 1 tablespoon + 2 teaspoons ground sweet paprika
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- Fine sea salt
- 2 small yellow onions, halved and thinly sliced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, lightly toasted
- 8 oz / 1 heaping cup French green lentils (uncooked), rinsed
- 2 cups chicken stock
- ¾ cup sour cream
- ½ cup finely chopped chives
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh dill, plus 1 tablespoon picked leaves for serving
- 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- ½ teaspoon granulated sugar

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Heat oven to 450°F.

**STEP 2:** In a large bowl, toss chicken, squash, 1 tablespoon paprika, 1 tablespoon oil, and 1 teaspoon salt. Set aside.

**STEP 3:** Place onions, 2 garlic cloves, ½ teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon salt, remaining 2 tablespoons oil, and 2 teaspoons paprika in a 9×13-inch baking dish. Mix to combine. Roast 10 minutes, stirring halfway, until onions are lightly colored. Stir in lentils, chicken stock, and 1 cup water. Arrange squash wedges and chicken (skin-side up) on top. Roast another 20 minutes until chicken skin is deeply golden. Turn oven down to 350°F and cook another 40 minutes, until chicken is cooked through and squash is nicely softened.

**STEP 4:** Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine sour cream, chives, and chopped dill. Set aside.

**STEP 5 — Make the daqa:** Mix remaining garlic and cumin with vinegar, sugar, ½ teaspoon salt, and 2 tablespoons water.

**STEP 6:** Remove from oven. Top with spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture. Pour daqa over everything. Sprinkle with picked dill leaves. Serve hot, straight from the baking dish.

---

## Notes

- **Daqa** is a Yemeni-inspired garlic-vinegar sauce — bright, sharp, and cuts through the richness of the chicken and sour cream beautifully; don't skip it
- **Kabocha squash** is the move here — holds its shape during the long roast and has a naturally sweet, nutty flavor; butternut or delicata work as substitutes
- **French green lentils (Puy)** hold their shape through roasting — don't sub red or yellow lentils, they'll turn to mush
- **Start at 450°F, finish at 350°F** — the high heat sets the skin crispy, then the low heat cooks the lentils through without drying out the chicken
- **One dish, no extra pots** — lentils cook directly in the stock in the baking dish; true one-pan cleanup
- **Meal-prep:** Keeps 4 days refrigerated; reheat covered at 325°F with a splash of stock to revive the lentils
- **Lighten it up:** Remove chicken skin before eating to drop ~80 cal and significant sat fat; swap sour cream for Greek yogurt to add protein and cut fat further
