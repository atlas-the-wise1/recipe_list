# One-Pan Chicken With Peperonata and Olives

**Category:** Dinner
**Tags:** #heart-healthy #high-protein #low-cholesterol #fat-loss #keto
**Servings:** 4
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 45 min | **Total:** ~60 min

---

## Ingredients

- 2 medium red bell peppers (~1 lb), cored, seeded and sliced ¼-inch-thick
- 2 medium yellow bell peppers (~1 lb), cored, seeded and sliced ½-inch-thick
- 5 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 3 thyme sprigs
- 5 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- Kosher salt and ground pepper
- 10 oz cherry or grape tomatoes (~2 cups)
- 4 oz pitted green olives, coarsely chopped (~¼ cup)
- 1 tsp red wine vinegar
- 1½ to 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- Chopped chives, for garnish

---

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to **425°F**. In a 9×13-inch or 3-quart baking dish, combine bell peppers, garlic, thyme, and 4 tbsp olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, toss to coat, and arrange in an even layer. Roast until softened, **~20 minutes**. Stir in tomatoes, olives, and vinegar.
2. In a large bowl, toss chicken with remaining 1 tbsp olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Arrange on top of the pepper mixture. Roast until chicken is golden and cooked through and peperonata is tender and saucy, **~25 minutes**. Discard thyme sprigs.
3. Divide chicken among plates, top with peperonata and pan juices. Sprinkle with chives.

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Storage:** 4 days fridge / 2 months freezer
**Reheat:** Oven at 350°F for 10–12 min or microwave covered with a splash of water
**Batch size:** Doubles easily in a larger roasting pan
**Pairs well with:** Crusty bread, brown rice, cauliflower rice (keto), or polenta
**Notes:** One of the simplest and most hands-off recipes in the book — mostly just chopping and roasting. The peperonata (slow-roasted peppers with tomatoes and olives) is the star here — sweet, tangy, and deeply savory. Bell peppers are loaded with vitamin C. Hits every health goal with minimal effort. The pan juices are worth every drop — spoon generously over the chicken.
