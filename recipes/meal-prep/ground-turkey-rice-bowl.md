# Ground Turkey & Brown Rice Meal Prep Bowl

**Category:** Meal Prep
**Tags:** `#high-protein` `#low-cholesterol` `#fat-loss` `#meal-prep` `#heart-healthy`
**Servings:** 1 bowl (scales to any batch size)
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** ~35 min

---

**Nutrition (per serving — with cotija/feta swap):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~490 | ~38g | ~16g | ~38g | ~7g | ~90mg |

*(With Mexican blend shredded cheese: ~505 cal, ~37g protein, ~103mg chol)*

---

## Ingredients (per bowl)

- 4 oz ground turkey (93/7 lean), cooked and seasoned
- ½ cup brown rice, cooked
- ¼ cup corn (frozen/canned, drained)
- ½ cup green beans (steamed or roasted)
- ¼ cup carrots (diced, steamed or roasted)
- 2 tbsp cotija or crumbled feta *(upgrade from Mexican blend — more flavor, less sat fat)*
- ¼ avocado, sliced

**Seasoning for turkey:**
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp chili powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Splash of lime juice

---

## Instructions

**Turkey:**
1. Brown ground turkey in a skillet over medium-high heat, breaking apart.
2. Season with cumin, garlic powder, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Finish with a squeeze of lime. Cook until no pink remains (~8 min). Set aside.

**Rice:**
- Cook brown rice per package instructions. Season lightly with salt.

**Veggies:**
- Option A (quick): Steam corn, green beans, and carrots together in microwave-safe bowl with 2 tbsp water, 3–4 min.
- Option B (better flavor): Roast at 400°F for 15–18 min with olive oil spray and a pinch of salt.

**Assembly:**
- Rice base → turkey → veggies → cotija/feta → avocado last (add fresh day-of for best texture).

---

## Meal Prep Notes

**Batch cooking (ET's method — office weekly prep):**
- Scale to however many bowls needed (4–5 for the week)
- Cook turkey in bulk, season uniformly
- Store rice, turkey, and veggies together in meal-prep containers
- **Store avocado separately** — add fresh each day (prevents browning)
- Cotija/feta holds up well in containers (unlike shredded melt-cheese which can get greasy)

**Storage:** 4–5 days refrigerated. Do not freeze assembled bowls (rice texture suffers).

**Reheat:** Microwave 2 min, stir halfway. Add avocado and cheese after reheating.

---

## Cheese Upgrade Note

**Cotija** (2 tbsp): ~45 cal / 3g protein / 12mg chol — punchy, salty, crumbles beautifully  
**Feta** (2 tbsp): ~50 cal / 3g protein / 13mg chol — tangy, lower sat fat than cheddar  
**Mexican blend** (2 tbsp): ~57 cal / 4g protein / 18mg chol — familiar flavor, higher sat fat  

Cotija or feta give you more flavor per calorie with a cleaner cholesterol profile. Either works.

---

## Variations

- **Add heat:** Jalapeños, hot sauce, or chipotle powder in turkey seasoning
- **Swap rice:** Cauliflower rice saves ~90 cal and 20g carbs if going lower-carb
- **Boost protein:** Add ¼ cup black beans (+7g protein, 0mg chol)
- **Swap veggie:** Broccoli, roasted zucchini, or bell peppers all work with this seasoning profile
