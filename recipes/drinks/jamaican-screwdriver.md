# Jamaican Screwdriver

**Category:** D — Drinks
**Tags:** `#cocktail` `#quick` `#vegan` `#low-cholesterol`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~345 | ~1g | ~0g | ~37g | ~2g | ~0mg |
**Serves:** 2–3
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 4 ounces vodka
- 2 ounces light rum
- 2 cups orange juice
- 1 cup frozen pineapple
- 2 cups ice
- 4 orange slices, for garnish

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above, except the orange slices.

**STEP 2:** Blend on HIGH until smooth.

**STEP 3:** Pour into chilled glasses and garnish with orange slices. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Double spirit:** Vodka + rum makes this stronger than it tastes — the OJ and pineapple mask the alcohol well; drink accordingly
- **Frozen pineapple** adds thickness and keeps it slushy; fresh pineapple + extra ice also works
- **Fresh OJ** is noticeably better here — the citrus is the main flavor driver; bottled works but fresh is worth it
- **Chilled glasses:** Pop glasses in the freezer 10 min before serving for the best slushy texture
- **Mocktail:** Skip the spirits, add ½ cup coconut water — tropical OJ-pineapple slushie
