# Toasted Almond Joy

**Category:** D — Drinks
**Tags:** `#cocktail` `#quick` `#vegetarian`
**Nutrition (per serving):**
| Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs | Fiber | Cholesterol |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~378 | ~3g | ~15g | ~44g | ~0g | ~37mg |
**Serves:** 3–4
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Total:** ~5 min

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup chocolate ice cream
- 1 cup vanilla ice cream
- 3 tablespoons cream of coconut
- 3 ounces amaretto liqueur
- 2 ounces dark crème de cacao
- ½ cup almond milk
- ½ cup ice

---

## Preparation

**STEP 1:** Place all ingredients into the blender pitcher in the order listed above.

**STEP 2:** Blend on HIGH until smooth. Serve immediately.

---

## Notes

- **Cream of coconut ≠ coconut milk** — use Coco López or similar sweetened cream of coconut; it's thick and sweet and drives the candy-bar flavor
- **Serve fast:** Ice cream melts quickly once blended; have glasses ready before you hit blend
- **Dessert cocktail:** At ~378 cal this drinks like dessert — treat it as one; not a session drink
- **Lighter version:** Swap ice cream for frozen banana (1 each) + 2 tbsp cocoa powder — cuts ~150 cal and all the cholesterol while keeping the chocolate-coconut profile
